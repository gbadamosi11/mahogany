How to respond to being scouted

Please only submit NATURAL photographs, no professional studio photos are required.
Please do not wear make-up, smile, or pout, just keep a straight face. Hair must e naturally styled, especially if you have curly or afro hair. Headshot image must be taken front-on and should fully show your face, portrait and full-length image should be taken with fitted clothing in these pictures, so that we can see your body shape well. Stand straight in the polaroid images and be natural.

for boys age 16-23, min height 6'1/185.5cm and girls, age 16-23, min height 5'9/176cm. We reserve the rights of only replying to submissions we want to meet, if you have not recieved a reply requesting you to attend a casting at the agency, unfortunately you have not been successful
